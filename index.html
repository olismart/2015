About this site

This site is an experiment in 'designing in the browser' - I'm building it as I go, without any prior planning or sketching. Or, for that matter, any idea what I really want to make in the end. It's also a sandbox for me to test new coding ideas and techniques, and scope out some of the newer web technologies. All without the worry of breaking a client project.

The whole thing is on github, so if you're interested you can take a look at how it's evolved.
